## Power budget

This document outlines the worst case power requirements of each component grouped by the required voltage. The totals will help guide regulator selection.

## 3.3V
- STM32F405
- MPU6000: 10mA
- LEDs: 20mA
- Satellite Reciever: 50mA

## 5V
- MAX7456: 160mA
- SBUS Inverter
- Buzzer
- RC Receiever: 50mA

## 12V
- HS1177: 50mA
- vTX: 350mA
